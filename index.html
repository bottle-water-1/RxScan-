Clear Results You Can Trust

Get one of three instant results for every verification

✅ Genuine

The drug is registered, safe, and approved by NAFDAC

You'll see:
  • Drug name & manufacturer
  • Registration date
  • Expiry status

❌ Not Found

The number doesn't match any NAFDAC record

This could mean:
  • Fake or counterfeit drug
  • Unregistered product
  • Incorrect number entry

⚠ Expired

Registration has expired - no longer approved for use

What this means:
  • Previously registered
  • Approval has lapsed
  • Not safe to use
